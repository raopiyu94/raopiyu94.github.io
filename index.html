Ivory Meetings

The website you're envisioning could be a sleek and user-friendly platform designed for managing and sharing appointments on behalf of others, like family members, employees, or clients. Here's a breakdown of how it might function:

Homepage:

Introduction: A clean, modern interface with a brief introduction highlighting the service's benefits—allowing users to book appointments for others and share contact details via QR code.
Call-to-Action Buttons: Prominent buttons such as "Book an Appointment" and "Share Contact Info."
Sign-Up/Login: Simple sign-up and login options with support for both individual and business accounts.

Features: Appointment Booking Dashboard: Users can create profiles for others (e.g., children, clients, or team members).
Calendar integration: The dashboard includes a calendar view to see upcoming appointments. The user can choose the date and time for the appointment, as well as the person on whose behalf the appointment is being made.
Service Listings: A directory of available services (e.g., doctor visits, salon bookings, meetings) with filtering options.
Automated Reminders: Email/SMS notifications for upcoming appointments for both the booker and the booked person.
QR Code Sharing:
After creating an appointment, users can generate a QR code containing the appointment details (date, time, location, and contact info) that can be shared with others.
Recipients can scan the QR code to view details or even add the appointment directly to their own calendars.
Share Contact: Another feature where users can create and share a digital business card via QR code. The QR contains contact info, which others can scan and save to their devices.
Profile Management:
Users can maintain contact and profile information for multiple people.
Include personal information fields, such as name, contact number, email, and preferences.
Permission Control: Option to allow those whose appointments are managed to modify or cancel appointments.
Analytics and History:
Track past appointments and usage stats (e.g., most frequently used services, total appointments booked).
Allow users to export appointment history if needed for reporting or tracking purposes.
User Flow:
Create an Account or log in using email, phone number, or social media.
Add Profiles: Add the details of people for whom appointments will be booked.
Book an Appointment: Choose the person, select the service, pick the time and confirm.
Generate and Share QR Code: Share the appointment details via a scannable QR code.
Manage Appointments: View, edit, or cancel any upcoming bookings.
Additional Features:
Business Mode: A tailored version for businesses that allows a manager or assistant to manage team member schedules.
Sync with Google Calendar: Let users sync all appointments to personal or work calendars.
Mobile App Integration: Provide a mobile app for easier QR code scanning and appointment management.
Technology:
QR Code Generator: This would use a third-party API for generating QR codes or could be built in-house using libraries like qrcode.js for JavaScript.
Calendar Integration: Use APIs such as Google Calendar API for seamless integration.
This platform could be ideal for personal use or businesses needing a streamlined approach to handling appointment bookings and contact sharing.
